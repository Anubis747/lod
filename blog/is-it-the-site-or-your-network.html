Is the Problem With the Site or Your Network?

Sometimes you try to open a website, and it just won’t load. But is the site actually down, or is the problem on your end? Here’s how you can figure it out.

Step 1: Try Another Website

If other websites load fine, the issue is probably with the specific site you're trying to visit. If no websites work, the problem is likely your connection.

Step 2: Restart Your Router

Internet issues are often solved with a simple router reboot. It clears up temporary connection glitches and resets your network settings.

Step 3: Test With Mobile Data

If you're on Wi-Fi, try opening the site using mobile data. If it works on mobile, the problem is with your local network or ISP.

Step 4: Use Live or Dead

Tools like Live or Dead help you confirm the site’s actual status. Just type the URL and check — if it's live for others but not for you, the issue is local.
