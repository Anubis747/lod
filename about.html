About This Tool

This simple tool helps you check if a website is currently online or down.

It's designed for anyone who needs a quick and accurate way to verify site availability, without installing plugins or extensions.

Whether you're a casual user, a developer, or someone managing services, Live or Dead saves you time and gives clarity.

We do not store or track any URLs entered. Everything is processed live and anonymously.
